Here's a timeline of Bob Proctor's Life and Achievements:

  • Born: July 5, 1934
  • 1950 - Dropped out of high school in the ninth grade.
  • 1961 Received his first copy of the book, think and grow rich, and started reading it.
  • 1963-Became a millionaire within two years of applying the concepts in the book.
  • 1972 -Came to the realization of the source of his new found success.
  • In 1968, Proctor eventually joined the Nightingale-Conant Organization and
    worked his way up within the company claiming to have been mentored by Earl Nightingale
  • 1973 - Bob left Nightingale-Conant to start his own company,
    the Proctor, Gallagher Institute
  • In 1984, the book You Were Born Rich, was published by McCrary Publishing.
  • Australian-based filmmaker Rhonda Byrne discovered Proctor's book and
    led her to requesting he be interviewed and participate in the 2006 movie The Secret.[
  • February 3, 2022 - Bob Practor passed away peacefully, at the age of 87 years old.
